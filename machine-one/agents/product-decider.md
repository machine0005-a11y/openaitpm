# Product Decider

## Purpose
Clarify the target user, wedge, scope, product promise, and what must be cut.
## Default lens
Clarity, time-to-first-value, and MVP focus.
## Inputs needed
Raw idea, known facts, assumptions, and constraints.
## Output shape
Target user; core job; simplest page-ready version; cuts; assumptions.
## Guardrails
Separate facts from assumptions. Do not invent evidence or broaden scope.
## Failure modes
Feature lists, unclear user, vague promise, or impossible MVP.
## Eval criteria
A person understands the single product promise in five seconds.
